Programs

Battle of the Brains Competition(2025)

I had the incredible opportunity to represent my university for the first time at the 8th Annual HBCU Battle of the Brains, a prestigious 24-hour hackathon-style competition that brought together students from 25 Historically Black Colleges and Universities across the nation. As a first-time participant, I collaborated with a dynamic team of students from diverse backgrounds to tackle a real-world digital advertising challenge.

Our task was to develop an innovative and sustainable solution to improve digital advertising. We proposed AmazonWish, a cutting-edge application that allows users to try out virtual models of products from the Amazon Marketplace before making a purchase. This interactive experience enabled us to design hyper-personalized advertisements that reduce ad waste and enhance customer satisfaction.

Despite it being my school's first time participating, my team proudly secured third place among 25 amazing HBCUs that participated, a milestone that showcased our innovation, technical expertise, and ability to collaborate effectively under pressure.

TMCF DevCon Conference(2025)

I had the incredible opportunity to attend the TMCF DevCon Conference, a three-day immersive experience designed for early-career students like myself. The conference provided an invaluable space to grow professionally, connect with inspiring peers and mentors, and gain clarity on potential career pathways.

Through a variety of interactive workshops, main stage presentations, and personalized coaching sessions, I was able to strengthen key skills such as effective communication, leadership, problem-solving, and networking. I engaged in thought-provoking discussions with top industry leaders and learned practical strategies for navigating the evolving landscape of the workforce.

DevCon not only equipped me with tools and insights to advance my career journey, but it also instilled a deeper sense of confidence and purpose. Being surrounded by driven, like-minded students and professionals reminded me of the importance of intentional growth, mentorship, and community support. The experience left me feeling empowered, inspired, and ready to take on new opportunities with clarity and determination.
